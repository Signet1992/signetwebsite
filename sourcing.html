Responsible Sourcing

Responsible sourcing.
Embedded in our production network.

Since 1992, Signet has managed apparel production through its established factory network across Asia. Responsible sourcing is embedded within this production network and supported by dedicated in-country sourcing, production, and compliance teams.

Responsible sourcing at Signet operates as part of how production is managed every day.

Responsible Production Framework

Integrated across our factory network.

Responsible sourcing is integrated across our factory network and supported by defined standards, in-country presence, and continuous oversight.

This framework focuses on four core areas — each embedded into how production is managed across our network on a daily basis.

Four Core Areas

How we maintain responsible production standards.

👷
Ethical Labour Standards

Production across our factory network operates in accordance with recognised social compliance standards. Working conditions are expected to meet established requirements, maintained through ongoing production oversight and long-term operational relationships.

  • Safe and hygienic working environments
  • Responsible working hours
  • Fair wages
  • No forced labour
  • No child labour
🌿
Environmental Responsibility

Environmental responsibility is integrated into production across our network. Facilities operate in accordance with recognised environmental standards and maintain appropriate controls, supporting responsible and sustainable garment production.

  • Responsible water and chemical management
  • Waste management practices
  • Environmental compliance systems
  • Continuous improvement programmes
🔍
Transparency Through Operational Oversight

Transparency is achieved through direct production oversight. Production visibility is maintained across our factory network through in-country sourcing, merchandising, and quality control teams — ensuring compliance requirements and responsible sourcing expectations are consistently upheld.

📈
Continuous Improvement

Responsible sourcing is an ongoing operational priority. Production across our network continues to evolve alongside regulatory requirements, customer expectations, and industry standards. Long-term operational relationships support the consistent application and strengthening of responsible production practices.

Responsible Sourcing in Practice

Delivered through daily operational involvement.

Responsible sourcing is delivered through daily operational involvement across our factory network — not through periodic audits alone.

  • Factory approval prior to production
  • Continuous production oversight
  • In-country quality control presence
  • Production monitoring throughout manufacturing
  • Compliance visibility and documentation

This integrated structure ensures responsible sourcing is maintained throughout the production lifecycle.

Certifications & Standards

Internationally recognised frameworks.

Production across our factory network operates within internationally recognised certification frameworks, depending on customer requirements.

🔷
SEDEX

Supplier Ethical Data Exchange — supporting ethical trade and responsible sourcing across global supply chains.

🌿
OEKO-TEX

Internationally recognised standard for textiles tested for harmful substances — ensuring product safety across all categories.

🌱
GOTS

Global Organic Textile Standard — ensuring organic fibres are handled responsibly throughout the entire supply chain.

✅
BSCI

Business Social Compliance Initiative — assessing and improving working conditions across global supply chains.

⭐
ISO Standards

International Organisation for Standardisation frameworks — supporting quality management and operational consistency across our network.

This structure provides global brands with confidence in both production and compliance.
