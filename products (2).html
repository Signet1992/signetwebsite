Products & Services

Apparel sourcing and production management in Asia.

Signet manages apparel sourcing and production for global brands through its established factory network across Asia, with core manufacturing capability in Bangladesh and Pakistan.

For over three decades, we have provided structured production management, quality control, and supply chain oversight — supporting brands that require reliable garment manufacturing with full operational transparency.

Our in-country sourcing, development, and quality control teams ensure production operates to defined standards from initial design through to final shipment. This integrated approach provides brands with confidence in quality, compliance, and delivery.

Services

Production managed at every stage.

Signet manages the full apparel sourcing and production lifecycle through its factory network — from initial design through to final shipment.

Stage 01
Design, Development & Sourcing

Garments are developed for production through structured design and technical development processes, with production requirements translated into manufacturing-ready products.

  • Design interpretation and refinement
  • Technical garment development
  • Fabric and trim sourcing
  • Sampling and prototype development
  • Material sourcing and cost optimisation
  • Technical specification alignment
This ensures garments are fully prepared and production-ready before manufacturing begins.
Stage 02
Production Allocation & Planning

Production is allocated within Signet's factory network in Bangladesh, Pakistan, and Asia based on defined criteria.

  • Technical capability
  • Product category
  • Production capacity
  • Compliance standards
This ensures production operates in the most appropriate facility.
Stage 03
Production Management

Production is overseen throughout manufacturing by in-country teams.

  • Production visibility
  • Timeline management
  • Operational control
This ensures production progresses according to plan.
Stage 04
Quality Control & Inspection

Quality is managed throughout production to ensure garments meet defined standards.

  • Inline production oversight
  • Final inspection
  • Quality verification
This protects product quality and consistency.
Stage 05
Compliance & Responsible Production

Production operates within recognised compliance and responsible sourcing frameworks.

  • Social compliance
  • Environmental standards
  • Certification requirements
This ensures production meets global brand expectations.
Stage 06
Shipment & Delivery Management

Shipment is managed to ensure reliable and timely delivery.

  • Export coordination
  • Documentation management
  • Delivery monitoring
This ensures garments move efficiently through the supply chain.
