About Signet

Three decades of production expertise.

Since 1992, Signet has managed apparel production through its factory network across Asia, supported by dedicated in-country sourcing, merchandising, and quality control teams.

Production operates within a defined framework that ensures quality, compliance, and delivery standards are maintained at every stage. Our infrastructure provides global brands with confidence, transparency, and operational reliability.

  • Established factory network developed over three decades
  • Dedicated in-country production and quality control teams
  • Responsible sourcing and compliance oversight
  • Long-term operational infrastructure across Asia
