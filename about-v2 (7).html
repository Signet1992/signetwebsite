Our Story

Built on experience. Proven through production.

Signet was established in London in 1992 with a clear objective: to provide global apparel brands with a reliable and accountable sourcing partner.

As global production expanded across Asia, Signet established permanent sourcing and quality control teams to manage production directly within its factory network.

Over more than three decades, this production network has grown to support customers across workwear, sportswear, and fashion categories.

Today, Signet manages production, quality, and compliance across its network — allowing customers to focus on their brand and market growth.

Our Approach

Quality and control at every stage.

Signet manages the full production lifecycle through its factory network, ensuring every stage operates to defined standards.

  • Product development and sampling
  • Production planning and management
  • Inline and final quality control
  • Compliance oversight
  • Shipment coordination and delivery monitoring

Continuous operational oversight ensures issues are identified early and resolved efficiently — protecting quality and ensuring reliable delivery.
